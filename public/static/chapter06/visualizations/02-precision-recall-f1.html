Precision, Recall & F₁

Adjust the sliders to see how the harmonic mean (F₁) balances Precision and Recall. The Venn diagram shows the relationship between Retrieved and Relevant sets.

💡
Try This: Set P=1.0 and R=0.1 — the F₁ drops drastically! Now try P=0.5 and R=0.5 — F₁ is higher even though both values are lower. Why? Because F₁ penalizes imbalance.
