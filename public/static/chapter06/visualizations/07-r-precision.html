Why R-Precision?

Unlike P@k where k is arbitrary, R-Precision uses the natural cutoff — the total number of relevant documents. This makes it self-adjusting: queries with more relevant documents get a larger evaluation window.

A perfect system that ranks all relevant documents above all non-relevant ones would achieve R-Precision = 1.0.
