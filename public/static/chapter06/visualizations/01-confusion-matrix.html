Formulas
Precision = TP / (TP + FP) — "Of what I retrieved, how much was relevant?"
Recall = TP / (TP + FN) — "Of all relevant docs, how many did I find?"
F₁ = 2·P·R / (P + R) — "Harmonic mean balancing both"
In IR, there are usually millions of non-relevant documents (TN is huge). A system that retrieves nothing still gets 99%+ accuracy! That's why IR uses Precision & Recall instead.
