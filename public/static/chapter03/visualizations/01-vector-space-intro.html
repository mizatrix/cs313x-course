🎯 Why Ranking Matters

Users want the BEST results first, not just all matches. Boolean gives no order — VSM ranks by similarity.

📊 Terms as Dimensions

Each unique word in the vocabulary becomes an axis. Documents are points in this high-dimensional space.

🔄 Feast or Famine

Boolean: either millions of results or zero. No middle ground. VSM solves this with continuous similarity scores.

📐 Geometric Intuition

Similar documents are CLOSE to each other in vector space. The query is also a vector — find the nearest documents!
