🏪 Search Results
A match in the title is more meaningful than a match in a long description. Field weighting lets the search engine prioritize where the match occurs:

• Title match (w=1.0): The product is specifically about those terms.
• Category match (w=0.8): The product belongs to the right category.
• Description match (w=0.5): The terms appear somewhere in the description — less specific.
